1- unzip SpriteLightKit-master.zip here
2- delete the ProjectSettings folder (it's only the Assets folder that you need)
3- add a new layer called LightLayer in Inspector/Layer(Default)/Add Layer
4- make sure the 
	SpriteLightKitImageEffect goes to your tk2dCamera camera
	SpriteLightKit goes to SpriteLightKit, child of tk2dCamera
